WILL
HYNES.

I'm a photographer and graphic designer based in Herefordshire, specialising in live music photography and gig poster design.

Most nights you'll find me in the pit at The Jam Factory or The Herdsman — shooting bands like Dreamwave, Yungold, Pollyanna Blue, and Dead On Arrival. I love the energy of live music, and I aim to capture that in my work.

On the design side, I make bold, print-ready posters for bands and promoters. Every poster is built around the music — not a template.
